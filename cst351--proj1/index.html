About

Hey, my names rick and I’m a freelance creative.

I’m based out of central California and am currently attending California State University Monterey Bay. I’m a web designer with a back end/database background, currently making my way into front end, and a big thing for me is I love to create stuff...

I’ve been a freelance creative since 2013, helping clients bring their creativity to light. Aside from shooting editorial, portraiture, events and real estate, i enjoy shooting astro, landscape, and lifestyle. Starting with photography, i’ve worked my way into video, graphics, and now web design; and because of these experiences I’ve acquired experience in Adobe Creative suites.

To couple my graphic and creative experience, I have experience with information systems, web development, application development, and relational database design.
