For project 3, we were tasked with creating a design metaphor.

To understand what it was I would be designing, I looked through past examples.

The challenge of this project was to find a way to create a design referencing color indirectly.

To do this, we began by brainstorming and developing mindmaps of things we felt related to the idea of color(s).

I personally felt that this was a fun, but confusing exercise. Though it was straight forward, the results were interesting.

After deciding on a few ideas we began design.

Here, I began designing prototype websites and mock-ups based on the ideas I'd come up with in the Ideation stage.

Of all the projects I was tasked with this semster, this one was the most enjoyable and where I felt I could see my growth the most. My designs here came most from a place of passion as I was able to design something from my heart with very minimal constraints.

After designing some mock-ups, my next step was to begin development of them using a digital tool.

Utilizing Adobe Illustrator, I began breathing life into my favorite design. Using sneakers as my inspiration, I was able to create a nire detailed representation of my sketches.

in doing this i provided myself a clear understanding of what makes up me, and this gave me quite a few ideas on where to start with designs.

The final step in this project was showing our final designs.

Here I added the finishing touches to my designs and finalized mock-ups showcasing design features, flow of content, and my design metaphor of relating sneakers, color, and music.

I am very proud of what I was able to create as a result of this project. I felt that this project was very much key in my development as a designer, and looking back at my past designs leading up to this, I can clearly see my growth.
